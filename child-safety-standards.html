Our commitment to protecting children from sexual abuse and exploitation

Our Commitment to Child Safety

At Oduaverse Tech Limited, we are committed to maintaining the highest standards for child safety and protection. We have zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) in any form.

Zero Tolerance Policy: We strictly prohibit any content, behavior, or activities that could harm, exploit, or endanger children in any way.

Our Safety Standards Include:

  • Content Moderation: All user-generated content is monitored and reviewed to ensure compliance with child safety policies
  • Age Verification: Appropriate age verification mechanisms are implemented where necessary
  • Reporting Mechanisms: Clear and accessible reporting channels for users to report suspected CSAE content or behavior
  • Staff Training: Regular training for all team members on child safety protocols and recognition of potential risks
  • Technology Safeguards: Implementation of advanced filtering and detection systems to prevent CSAE content
  • Third-Party Partnerships: Collaboration with recognized child safety organizations and law enforcement agencies

Prohibited Content and Activities:

  • Sexual exploitation of minors in any form
  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Grooming or predatory behavior targeting minors
  • Content that sexualizes, grooms, abuses, or otherwise exploits children
  • Facilitating inappropriate interactions between adults and minors

Detection and Response:

We employ both automated systems and human reviewers to detect and remove harmful content. When potential CSAE content is identified:

  • Content is immediately removed and accounts are suspended
  • Reports are made to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and relevant law enforcement agencies
  • Comprehensive investigations are conducted
  • Additional preventive measures are implemented as needed

User Education and Awareness:

We are committed to educating our users about online safety, including:

  • Providing resources on digital citizenship and online safety
  • Clear community guidelines that emphasize child protection
  • Regular updates on safety features and reporting tools
  • Partnerships with child safety organizations for awareness campaigns

Continuous Improvement:

Our child safety standards are regularly reviewed and updated to address emerging threats and incorporate best practices from industry leaders and child safety experts.

Reporting Concerns

If you encounter content or behavior that may violate our child safety standards, please report it immediately:

Email: safety@oduaverse.com

Emergency: Contact your local law enforcement or emergency services

NCMEC CyberTipline: www.missingkids.org/gethelpnow/cybertipline

Compliance and Partnerships:

We work closely with:

  • National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC)
  • Internet Watch Foundation (IWF)
  • Local and international law enforcement agencies
  • Child safety advocacy organizations
  • Technology safety coalitions

Our child safety standards are aligned with international best practices and comply with applicable laws and regulations in all jurisdictions where we operate.
