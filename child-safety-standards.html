Our Commitment to Child Safety

At Oduaverse Tech Limited, developers of OneVoice and other innovative applications, we are committed to maintaining the highest standards for child safety and protection. We have zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) in any form across all our platforms and applications.

Featured Application: OneVoice

OneVoice is our revolutionary communication platform that brings communities together with real-time messaging and collaboration. As a platform that facilitates communication, we maintain stringent child safety measures to ensure a safe environment for all users.

Zero Tolerance Policy: Across OneVoice and all Oduaverse applications, we strictly prohibit any content, behavior, or activities that could harm, exploit, or endanger children in any way.

Our Safety Standards for OneVoice and All Applications Include:

  • Real-Time Content Moderation: In OneVoice's messaging platform and all our applications, user-generated content is monitored in real-time and reviewed to ensure compliance with child safety policies
  • Age Verification: Appropriate age verification mechanisms are implemented across all platforms, with enhanced protections for communication features in OneVoice
  • Easy Reporting Mechanisms: Clear and accessible reporting channels within OneVoice and all applications for users to report suspected CSAE content or behavior
  • Comprehensive Staff Training: Regular training for all team members on child safety protocols and recognition of potential risks in communication platforms
  • Advanced Technology Safeguards: Implementation of AI-powered filtering and detection systems specifically designed for real-time communication platforms like OneVoice
  • Law Enforcement Partnerships: Collaboration with recognized child safety organizations and law enforcement agencies for immediate response
  • Communication Safety: Special focus on preventing predatory behavior in messaging and community features within OneVoice

Prohibited Content and Activities:

  • Sexual exploitation of minors in any form
  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Grooming or predatory behavior targeting minors
  • Content that sexualizes, grooms, abuses, or otherwise exploits children
  • Facilitating inappropriate interactions between adults and minors

Detection and Response (OneVoice & All Applications):

We employ both automated systems and human reviewers to detect and remove harmful content across OneVoice's real-time communications and all our applications. When potential CSAE content is identified:

  • Content is immediately removed and accounts are suspended across all platforms
  • In OneVoice, chat logs and communication history are preserved for law enforcement
  • Immediate reports are made to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and relevant law enforcement agencies
  • Comprehensive investigations are conducted with special focus on communication patterns in OneVoice
  • Additional preventive measures and enhanced monitoring are implemented as needed
  • User safety notifications are sent when appropriate

User Education and Awareness:

We are committed to educating our users about online safety, including:

  • Providing resources on digital citizenship and online safety
  • Clear community guidelines that emphasize child protection
  • Regular updates on safety features and reporting tools
  • Partnerships with child safety organizations for awareness campaigns

Continuous Improvement:

Our child safety standards are regularly reviewed and updated to address emerging threats and incorporate best practices from industry leaders and child safety experts.

Reporting Concerns - OneVoice & All Applications

If you encounter content or behavior in OneVoice, our other applications, or any of our platforms that may violate our child safety standards, please report it immediately:

OneVoice In-App Reporting: Use the built-in reporting feature within the OneVoice app

Email: safety@oduaverse.com

Emergency: Contact your local law enforcement or emergency services

NCMEC CyberTipline: www.missingkids.org/gethelpnow/cybertipline

Compliance and Partnerships:

We work closely with:

  • National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC)
  • Internet Watch Foundation (IWF)
  • Local and international law enforcement agencies
  • Child safety advocacy organizations
  • Technology safety coalitions

Our child safety standards for OneVoice and all Oduaverse applications are aligned with international best practices and comply with applicable laws and regulations in all jurisdictions where we operate. These standards are specifically enhanced for communication platforms like OneVoice to address the unique risks associated with real-time messaging and community features.
